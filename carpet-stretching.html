What is Carpet Re-stretching?

Carpet re-stretching is a professional service that eliminates wrinkles, bumps, and loose areas in your carpet. Over time, carpets can become loose and develop unsightly wrinkles that not only look bad but can also be a tripping hazard. Our expert technicians use specialized equipment to properly stretch and re-secure your carpet, restoring its original appearance and safety.

Our Carpet Stretching Services Include:

Wrinkle Removal

Eliminate unsightly wrinkles and ripples that develop over time with professional stretching techniques.

Bump Elimination

Remove carpet bumps and lumps that can cause tripping hazards and affect appearance.

Proper Tensioning

Ensure your carpet is properly tensioned for optimal performance and longevity.

Extended Carpet Life

Proper stretching extends the life of your carpet and prevents premature wear.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our carpet stretching services

How long does carpet stretching take?

Most stretching jobs can be completed within 2-4 hours depending on the size of the area and complexity of the work.

Do I need to move my furniture?

We can move most furniture for you, but we recommend moving small items and valuables before we arrive.

How often should carpet be stretched?

Carpet typically needs re-stretching every 5-10 years, depending on usage and installation quality.

Can you stretch any type of carpet?

We can stretch most types of carpet, including loop pile, cut pile, and textured carpets.

Will stretching damage my carpet?

No, professional stretching actually extends carpet life and prevents damage from wrinkles and loose areas.

What areas do you service?

We provide carpet stretching services throughout Melbourne and surrounding suburbs. Contact us to confirm service in your area.
