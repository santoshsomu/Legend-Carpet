What is Professional Upholstery Cleaning?

Professional upholstery cleaning is a specialized service that deep cleans and restores your furniture fabrics. Over time, upholstery accumulates dirt, dust, allergens, and stains that regular vacuuming can't remove. Our expert technicians use advanced cleaning techniques and fabric-safe solutions to restore your furniture to its original beauty while extending its life.

Our Upholstery Cleaning Services Include:

Fabric-Safe Cleaning

Gentle yet effective cleaning methods that are safe for all fabric types and colors.

Stain Removal

Professional stain removal techniques for food spills, pet stains, and other difficult marks.

Odor Elimination

Remove pet odors, smoke smells, and other unpleasant odors with specialized treatments.

Protection Treatment

Optional fabric protection to help prevent future stains and spills.

Allergen Removal

Remove dust mites, pet dander, and other allergens that can cause health issues.

Fast Drying

Advanced extraction methods ensure furniture dries quickly, typically within 2-4 hours.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our upholstery cleaning services

How often should I have my upholstery cleaned?

We recommend professional cleaning every 12-18 months for most furniture, or every 6-12 months for high-use items.

How long does upholstery cleaning take?

Most jobs take 2-4 hours depending on the size and number of pieces being cleaned.

How long does it take for furniture to dry?

Furniture typically dries within 2-4 hours with our advanced extraction methods and proper ventilation.

Can you clean all types of fabric?

Yes, we can clean most fabric types. We test cleaning solutions first to ensure safety and effectiveness.

Are your cleaning solutions safe for pets and children?

Yes, we use eco-friendly, non-toxic cleaning solutions that are safe for children and pets.

Do you move furniture during cleaning?

We can move most furniture to access all areas. We recommend moving small items and valuables before we arrive.
