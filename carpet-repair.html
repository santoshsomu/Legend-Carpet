What is Carpet Repair & Patching?

Carpet repair and patching is a specialized service that restores damaged areas of your carpet to their original condition. Whether you have burn marks, tears, holes, or stubborn stains, our expert technicians can seamlessly repair these issues, often making the damage completely invisible.

Our Carpet Repair Services Include:

Burn Mark Repair

Remove cigarette burns, iron marks, and other heat damage with precision patching techniques.

Tear & Hole Patching

Repair tears, holes, and cuts in your carpet with seamless patching that matches perfectly.

Stain Removal

Remove stubborn stains that regular cleaning couldn't eliminate with advanced techniques.

Seam Repair

Fix loose or damaged carpet seams to prevent further deterioration and improve appearance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our carpet repair services

How long does carpet repair take?

Most repairs can be completed within 1-2 hours. Complex repairs may take longer, but we'll provide an accurate timeline during assessment.

Will the repair be visible?

Our expert technicians use advanced techniques to make repairs virtually invisible. We match color, texture, and pattern perfectly.

What types of damage can you repair?

We can repair burn marks, tears, holes, stains, loose seams, and most other carpet damage. Contact us for a free assessment.

Do you offer emergency repair services?

Yes, we offer emergency repair services for urgent situations. Call us anytime for immediate assistance.

Is carpet repair covered by insurance?

Many insurance policies cover carpet repair, especially for damage from accidents or natural disasters. We can help with insurance claims.

What areas do you service?

We provide carpet repair services throughout Melbourne and surrounding suburbs. Contact us to confirm service in your area.
