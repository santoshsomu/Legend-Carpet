What is Professional Carpet Installation?

Professional carpet installation is more than just laying carpet on the floor. It involves precise measurement, proper subfloor preparation, expert cutting and fitting, and professional finishing techniques. Our installation service ensures your new carpet is perfectly fitted, properly secured, and will provide years of comfort and beauty.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our carpet installation services

How long does carpet installation take?

Most installations take 1-2 days depending on the size of the area and complexity of the job.

Do you provide carpet samples?

Yes, we can provide samples of various carpet styles and colors to help you make the best choice.

Can you install carpet over existing flooring?

In most cases, yes. We'll assess the condition of existing flooring and prepare accordingly.

What warranty do you provide?

We provide comprehensive warranty coverage for both installation work and materials.

Do you move furniture during installation?

Yes, we can move most furniture. We recommend moving small items and valuables before we arrive.

What areas do you service?

We provide carpet installation services throughout Melbourne and surrounding suburbs.
