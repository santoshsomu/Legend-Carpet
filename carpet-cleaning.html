Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our carpet cleaning services

How often should I have my carpets cleaned?

We recommend professional cleaning every 12-18 months for most homes, or every 6-12 months for high-traffic areas or homes with pets.

How long does carpet cleaning take?

Most jobs take 2-4 hours depending on the size of the area and the level of soiling.

How long does it take for carpets to dry?

Carpets typically dry within 2-4 hours with our advanced extraction methods and proper ventilation.

Can you remove all stains?

We can remove most stains, but some permanent damage may not be completely reversible. We'll assess each stain individually.

Are your cleaning solutions safe for pets and children?

Yes, we use eco-friendly, non-toxic cleaning solutions that are safe for children and pets.

Do you move furniture?

We can move most furniture, but we recommend moving small items and valuables before we arrive.

Steam vs. Dry Cleaning: What's the difference?

Steam cleaning (hot water extraction) provides a deeper, more thorough clean that removes embedded soil and sanitizes carpets. Dry cleaning is a lower-moisture option for delicate fibers or when very fast drying is essential.

Is steam cleaning safe for my type of carpet?

Our technicians are trained to identify different carpet fibers (wool, nylon, polyester) and adjust the temperature, pressure, and cleaning solutions to ensure a safe and effective clean for your specific carpet type.
